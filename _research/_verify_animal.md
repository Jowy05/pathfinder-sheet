## Item 1: Witch hexes "Animal Skin" / "Animal Servant"
- Source: https://aonprd.com/WitchHexes.aspx
- Original: `"**Witch** (hexes Animal Skin / Animal Servant) | ver hexes | [VERIFICAR] hex de gran poder"`
- Resolved:
  - **Animal Skin** is a **Major Hex**. Effect: the witch transforms herself into any animal of size Tiny–Large whose skin she wears (functions as *beast shape II*, takes the form of the specific animal). This is **personal shapeshifting, NOT an animal companion**.
  - **Animal Servant** is a **Grand Hex**. Effect: the witch transforms a humanoid enemy into an animal (via *beast shape II* effect) and gains mental domination over it (as *dominate monster*, one Will save, no further saves). This is **enemy control, NOT an animal companion**.
  - Neither hex grants a Witch an animal companion with companion progression. The Witch gets a **familiar** through class features, not a companion. There is no Witch hex that replicates the Druid animal companion mechanic.
- Action: Update research doc line 37 — clarify that Animal Skin (major hex) = self-transformation, Animal Servant (grand hex) = dominated enemy. Neither grants a companion animal. Remove the implication that these give the witch a companion; add note "Witch does not gain animal companion via hexes".

## Item 6: "Air Support" training package
- Source: https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/handle-animal/
- Original: `"**Air Support** | 20 | [VERIFICAR] | Attack, Bombard, Deliver"`
- Resolved:
  - **DC: 20** — confirmed.
  - **Tricks: Attack, Bombard, Deliver** — confirmed.
  - **Training time: NOT specified** in d20pfsrd. The page lists Air Support as a purpose but does NOT give a week count in the standard table. The formal table only lists: Combat Riding (6 wk), Fighting (3 wk), Guarding (4 wk), Heavy Labor (2 wk), Hunting (6 wk), Performance (5 wk), Riding (3 wk).
  - Air Support, Liberate, and Serve are listed as purposes but **without explicit week values** in the official d20pfsrd text.
  - **Canonicity:** Air Support is a canonical Paizo entry (appears on d20pfsrd Handle Animal page) but likely from a supplement (Ultimate Combat or similar), not Core Rulebook.
- Action: Update research doc — confirm DC 20 and tricks. Mark time as "not specified in rules text — use GM discretion or assume 3–4 weeks by analogy." Note source is non-Core supplement.

### 10e. Octopus (base stats)
- Research doc: 8 arms 1d3 + grab; Str 10, Dex 17, Con 12, Int 3; special: jet propulsion, low-light vision; 4th adv: [VERIFICAR]

### Wolf (bonus check)

- **Two discrepancies**: base Nat AC is +1 not +2; 4th advancement is only +Str 2/+Con 2 (no Dex change, no trip, no size change)

- **Discrepancies**: base Str/Dex/Con all wrong; base bite is 1d4 not 1d6; 4th-adv bite is 1d6 not 1d8; no nat AC bonus listed for dog 4th adv.
